A designer on our Pale Blue Dot.

From Edmonton, Alberta. Currently a junior studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

A Canadian designer quoting an American astronomer from the 90s might seem quite arbitrary. The truth is, I really liked that documentary about the Voyager expeditions.

However, as a designer, I owe you better justification than that, so I will tell you I like that the image of our Pale Blue Dot reminds us of our solitude in a vast universe. If the Pale Blue Dot is all we've got, then it deserves a little compassion. It is a reminder to design with integrity and to provide an explanation when it's warranted.

When I'm not designing or watching existentialist space documentaries, I'm searching up answers to NYT crosswords and figuring out how to dye my hair phoebe-bridgers-blonde.

I'm currently seeking Fall 2021 product design or research internships.
